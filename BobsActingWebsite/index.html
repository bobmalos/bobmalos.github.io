Bob Malos is an accomplished and award-winning voice and stage actor (and film too).  He was the narrator of the Emmy-winning 2010 documentary “Troubled Waters:  A Mississippi River Story”.  His voice was heard on several web videos on the Subaru website from 2011 to 2018. On stage he has performed in over 100 plays in professional theaters in the East and Midwest. In 2006 he was named “Best Male Actor” by the Minneapolis/St. Paul publication “City Pages”.

I recorded all the Audio Descriptions for Space: An Out-of-Gravity Experience at the Science Museum of Minnesota in St. Paul. This is a brand new $6 million exhibit that will be touring nationally after a run in St. Paul. I recorded about one hour of audio to cover the descriptions of all the objects/displays in the exhibit.
I’m very happy to be part of the world premiere of “The Red Box” at Park Square Theatre Company in St. Paul. This is the first time a Nero Wolfe mystery has been presented on stage. I’m one of the good guys – or – wait – am I really one of the bad guys?
